# DS_PBLE
Inventory for Electronics, Clothing and Groceries
**Electronic Inventory Management App**

Take full control of your electronic product inventory with a smart and efficient management system designed for simplicity and accuracy. This application allows users to seamlessly add, update, and remove product quantities while maintaining real-time stock visibility.

With an intuitive interface and structured category system, users can easily manage electronic items using unique product IDs and names. The system automatically updates stock levels when new units are added or removed, ensuring precise inventory tracking at all times.

A standout feature of this app is its intelligent stock alert mechanism. When attempting to delete more than 60% of the available quantity, the system provides a warning to prevent accidental over-depletion of stock. Additionally, items are automatically removed from the inventory once their quantity reaches zero, keeping your records clean and up to date.

**Key Features:**

* Add new electronic products or increase existing stock
* Delete product units with smart warning alerts
* Automatic stock updates and real-time tracking
* Organized category-based inventory system
* Automatic removal of out-of-stock items
* Simple and user-friendly interface

This application is ideal for small businesses, students, and developers looking to efficiently manage electronic inventory with minimal complexity and maximum reliability.
